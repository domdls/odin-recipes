Radicalization

Description

A personal favorite (and loved the world over), this recipe for Radicalization is sure to make a smash at your local potluck, fight club, office party, or clandestine political meeting!

Editor's Tip: this recipe can be very polarizing! Many people like to customize this recipe to their personal tastes by adding their own favorite flavors. If you find people getting upset by your tweaks, don't worry! This recipe serves as the base of the dish, and the base is popular with everyone. Just keep experimenting with the specifics, and before you know it, you'll have everybody hooked!

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups desire for significance, finely chopped
  • 28oz canned social alienation (Editor's Tip: homemade can work as well, just make sure it's at least a few days old so the flavors have time to mingle)
  • 1 heaping Tbsp inability to affect society
  • 6 fl oz desire for community
  • 3-4 large handfuls unresolved trauma
  • 1 large hatred for percieved "other"
  • 4 cups echo-chamber
  • Gunpowder (to taste)
  • 1 bunch parsley (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Combine alienation, desire for significance, and inability to affect society in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Swallow your unresolved trauma.
  3. Before adding echo-chamber to the bowl, spend at least six sleepless nights reinforcing your opinions on social media (the more the better).
  4. Beat ingredients to combine.
  5. Pour mixture into large saucepan and add enough water to cover by about an inch. Add gunpowder and heat over medium-high heat.
  6. When saucepan begins to boil over, reduce heat to medium-low and cover. Stir occasionally.
  7. Meanwhile, knead your hatred for the percieved other while until stiff and barely malleable. This may take some time. Put your back into it!
  8. Once simmering gunpowder mixture is about to blow, remove from heat and slowly pour over your rock-hard hatred.
  9. This is a good point to add in your own personal flair to this recipe! Remember: the specifics don't matter. Left, right, center, up, down, whatever! All that matters is that you make it violent and angry.
  10. Vomit your unresolved trauma all over everything.
  11. Garnish with parsley.
