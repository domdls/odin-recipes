Stagnation

Description

Just like mom used to make! A classic homestyle favorite, Stagnation is rich with subtle flavors designed to hypnotize and delight. Lots of people learn to make this from old family recipes, but if you don't have a good relationship with your parents, don't fret! This recipe is certain to satisfy your cravings, and with a little practice, you can settle into a routine that you'll be able to pass on yourself when the time is right!

Editor's Tip: be mindful of portion control with this one! If you make your servings too big, you'll be sure to end up in a food coma.

Ingredients

  • 1800 mg lithium, extended release
  • 20 mg escitalopram oxalate
  • 200 mg caffeine
  • 5 mg melatonin
  • 10 mg zolpidem tartrate
  • 6 cups corn syrup
  • 12 lbs mechanically separated beef
  • 36 slices American cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F
  2. Add all pills to large casserole dish.
  3. Cover with corn syrup. Use a large wooden spoon to mix.
  4. Continue mixing until you start to wonder if this is really all there is.
  5. Pour in the beef.
  6. Consider whether "poured" was ever a verb you wanted to see associated with "beef"
  7. Mix beef and pill/syrup slurry with the same wooden spoon.
  8. Tell yourself this really isn't that bad. After all, you've got a roof over your head, right?
  9. Layer the top of the casserole dish with the cheese.
  10. Bake for however long it takes.
  11. Consider smoking a cigarette, before reminding yourself that it's bad for you.
  12. Remove dish from oven.
  13. Eat the whole thing in one sitting. Tell yourself you love it. Maybe you do. How can you really be sure, anyway?
